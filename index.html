About Me

I am a master student at the University of Michigan in Robotics Institute. My main research interest is Computer Vision and Robotics Perception. I received my B.S.E. in Computer Science at the University of Michigan in 2019. I was previously a Mechanical Engineering student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the UM-SJTU Joint Institute.
